About me

Wenting Tan (Michelle)

Wenting Tan is a recent graduate with a M.S. degree in computer science at the College of William and Mary. She also got her M.S. and B.S. degrees in Physics. She currently is working as a software engineer in Silicon Valley. Art is also a part of her life. She plays piano and pipa and is very good at sketching.

She is an enthusiast in Astronomy, ancient civilizations, wild adventure and traveling...
